# Jargon

- **Tiles**: The "nodes" that are panels used to modify elements, and can be connected to each other.

- **Cables**: The ropes/wires that connect tiles.

- **Clips**: The "reroute nodes" that redirect and organize the cables of the tiles.

- **Elements**: The UI elements like rectangles, are being modified by the tiles.

- **Sockets**: The connection points on the tiles that are used to connect cables.
